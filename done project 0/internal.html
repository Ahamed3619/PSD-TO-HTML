Our Directors

Karen Kutter

karen@kutterwalters.co.uk

Karen has worked in legal costs since 2008, commencing work as a costs draftsman after completing her law degree. She subsequently completed the LPC at Guildford College of Law, obtaining a Distinction. She is a member of the Association of Costs Lawyers and is currently completing the 3 year Costs Lawyers course which she is due to complete in 2014. Karen has a broad experience in relation to many types of costs law. She has a 100% success rate when undertaking advocacy in the SCCO and in relation to challenging public funding assessments. She has conducted successful negotiations to achieve the best outcomes for clients. Karen has undertaken a number of urgent Precedent H costs budgets, ensuring documentation is always filed on time. Karen regularly costs large complex matters both legally aided (including High Costs Case Plans and Events Based Case Plans) and inter partes matters.
